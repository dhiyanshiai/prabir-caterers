## What Impacts Tent Decoration Cost?

### 1. Venue Size
Larger areas = more fabric, lighting, manpower.

### 2. Theme Selection
Popular Varanasi themes:
*   **Ganga Aarti theme**
*   **Rajwadi theme**
*   **Floral pastel themes**

Explore our [**Theme Decor Gallery**](/tent-house-varanasi) for inspiration.

### 3. Mandap Design
Mandap costs alone range from:
*   **₹40,000** (simple)
*   **₹2,00,000+** (custom floral)

### 4. Lighting & Furniture
Chandeliers, stage lighting, sofas, and dining setups significantly influence cost.
